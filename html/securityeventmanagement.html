It involves the use of various tools, techniques, and policies to ensure that security incidents are detected and responded to in a timely and effective manner. SEM encompasses a range of activities, including event detection, log management, threat intelligence sharing, incident response planning, and continuous improvement. Threat Intelligence Sharing: This involves the sharing of threat intelligence information between organizations, in order to improve overall security posture. Incident Response Planning: This involves the development of plans and procedures for responding to security incidents. This should include identification of key personnel, communication protocols, and the establishment of a robust incident response framework.
