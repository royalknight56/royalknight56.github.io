Wired connections provide a stable and fast connection, but they can be inconvenient as they require cable installations. 2. This provides an internet connection when traditional Wi-Fi is not available. 4. Bluetooth connectivity: Bluetooth technology allows devices to establish a wireless connection to exchange data.
