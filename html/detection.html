It is a fundamental concept in various fields, including science, technology, and everyday life. This can be done through the use of cameras, sensors, or even our eyes. Visual detection is used in a wide range of fields, including security, intelligence, and agriculture. Another common type of detection is sensor detection, which involves using sensors to detect specific substances or events. Sensors can be designed to detect a wide range of substances, including chemical compounds, biological molecules, and even radiological materials. Sensor detection is used in a variety of fields, including environmental monitoring, health care, and industrial automation.
